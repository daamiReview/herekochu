    Established in May 2020, www.daamireview.com is a simple movie review and rating portal with the vision to provide go-to details and supporting great movies. Our reviews feature a unique and influential ranking system that captures the different aspects of the movie-going experience with a various mindset. Our reviewers have nearly a decade of experience in movie analysis and criticism. The site consist of reviews of movies from different languages and genres. Primararily focused in promoting and providing meaningful review and snapshot of Nepali movies, we combine our effort to provide the cutting edge information of the movies that we review. We are trying our best to provide detailed analysis and synopsis of our chosen movies that will help our audience to choose a right movie for their entertainment and worth of time. Join us as we guide you through the treacherous world of reviews and movies. We believe in truth, entertainment and movies!

    Biwash is a Information Technology Analyst by profession. What can we say about Biwash? He loves movies, pure and simple! He is a movie enthusiasts who loves watching movies and series from different genre and languages. He has nearly over a decade of experience in film criticisms and analysis. Not long ago he coined what has become a “Buff” motto - “You watch a movie, it gets reviewed. Simple.” Biwash reviews a wide-breadth of movies from romantic, comedies, action/adventure, drama to horror and approaches reviews with the philosophy of their enjoyability to the casual film viewer. He asks such questions, as “Is it fun?” “Does the plot have holes?” “Would you watch it again?” "Would you refer this movie to a close friend?" simple and easy from a reviewer's point of view.
